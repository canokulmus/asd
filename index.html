Project Details

With online marketing being the primary shopping choice for most people, vendors are facing different problems. One of them is deciding a product's selling price. Vendors have many competitors on online marketing platforms, therefore they have to be smart about setting prices. If they set prices too high, they will fall behind their competitors and will not sell much; if they set them too low they will not make much profit. To find the optimal selling price, vendors need to use the prices of their competitors. With DPS (Dynamic Price Setting), we aim to provide a stand-alone rule-based dynamic price determination system for the chosen e-commerce product. It will use price and sale amount history provided by STOCKMOUNT and scraped from Trendyol and HepsiBurada to predict how much a product would sell if it is sold at a price given by the user.
